Welcome to India

India is a country in South Asia. It is known for its rich history, diverse culture, and vibrant traditions.

Geography

India is the seventh-largest country by land area, and the second-most populous country. It is bounded by the Indian Ocean, the Arabian Sea, and the Bay of Bengal.

Major Cities

  • New Delhi - The capital city of India.
  • Mumbai - Known as the financial capital of India.
  • Bengaluru - Known as the Silicon Valley of India.

Languages

India has 22 officially recognized languages, with Hindi and English being the most widely spoken.

Cultural Heritage

India is known for its diverse cultural heritage, including dance, music, art, and festivals.

Some famous cultural elements include:

  • Bharatanatyam - A classical dance form
  • Carnatic Music
  • Diwali - Festival of Lights
  • Holi - Festival of Colors

Tourist Attractions

Some popular tourist attractions in India include:

  1. Taj Mahal
  2. Jaipur
  3. Kerala
